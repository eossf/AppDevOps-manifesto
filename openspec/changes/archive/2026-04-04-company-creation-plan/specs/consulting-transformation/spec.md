## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Tier 3 Consulting Format
The consulting service SHALL provide on-demand consulting engagements at $1,200 per day.

#### Scenario: Consulting engagement
- **WHEN** a client requests Tier 3 consulting
- **THEN** engagements SHALL be priced at $1,200 per day
- **AND** minimum engagement SHALL be 2 days
- **AND** maximum team size SHALL be 2 consultants

### Requirement: Tier 3 Layer 3 Focus
The consulting SHALL cover Layer 3 content: AI strategy development, custom agent pipelines, integration architecture, and embedded coaching.

#### Scenario: AI strategy development
- **WHEN** a client needs to define their AI roadmap
- **THEN** consulting SHALL include current state assessment, AI opportunity identification, and prioritization framework
- **AND** SHALL produce a written AI strategy document

#### Scenario: Custom agent pipeline development
- **WHEN** a client needs specialized agents for their workflow
- **THEN** consulting SHALL include requirements gathering, architecture design, and implementation guidance
- **AND** SHALL deliver documented agent specifications

#### Scenario: Integration architecture
- **WHEN** a client needs to integrate AI into existing systems
- **THEN** consulting SHALL include system design, API specifications, and implementation roadmap
- **AND** SHALL address security, compliance, and scalability concerns

#### Scenario: Embedded coaching
- **WHEN** a client needs hands-on guidance during AI adoption
- **THEN** consulting SHALL include working alongside their teams
- **AND** SHALL provide code reviews, pair programming, and knowledge transfer sessions

### Requirement: Tier 3 Success Metrics
The consulting SHALL define measurable success metrics with the client before engagement begins.

#### Scenario: Success metrics definition
- **WHEN** a consulting engagement starts
- **THEN** measurable outcomes SHALL be documented and agreed upon
- **AND** baseline measurements SHALL be recorded
- **AND** final measurements SHALL be compared at engagement end

### Requirement: Consulting Documentation
The consulting SHALL produce detailed documentation for all deliverables.

#### Scenario: Documentation交付
- **WHEN** a consulting deliverable is completed
- **THEN** comprehensive documentation SHALL be provided
- **AND** SHALL include architecture diagrams, code samples, and operational runbooks
- **AND** SHALL be in English (or French if client prefers)
