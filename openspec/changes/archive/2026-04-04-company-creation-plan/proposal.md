## Why

Enterprises with 150+ employees are struggling to adopt AI in their development workflows. They have the tools (Copilot, Claude, etc.) but lack the methodology to integrate AI into a coherent workflow. Most training vendors focus on Layer 1 (tools), while enterprises need help with Layer 2-3 (integration and strategy).

This creates an opportunity to build a training and consulting company that:
- Teaches the "Feature Craft" methodology from the AppDevOps Manifesto
- Operates as a trusted advisor (not a vendor selling tools)
- Uses a tiered model: Tier 1-2 training as lead generation, Tier 3 consulting as the margin driver

## What Changes

**New Business Entity**:
- Training services (Tier 1: Foundation, Tier 2: Practitioner)
- Consulting services (Tier 3: Transformation)
- Thought leadership via AppDevOps Manifesto publication

**Revenue Model**:
| Tier | Type | Duration | Price |
|------|------|----------|-------|
| Tier 1 | Training | 1 day, 8 max | $400/person/day |
| Tier 2 | Training | 3 days, 6 max | $500/person/day |
| Tier 3 | Consulting | On-demand | $1,200/day |

**Market Entry Strategy**:
- Phase 1: Publish manifesto for thought leadership
- Phase 2: Build authority via LinkedIn and network
- Phase 3: Land first clients via warm contacts
- Phase 4: Scale via referrals and case studies

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `training-foundation`: Tier 1 training covering Layer 1 (AI tools basics, LLM fundamentals, prompt engineering, AI-assisted coding intro)
- `training-practitioner`: Tier 2 training covering Layer 2 (agent development, RAG systems, AI DevOps integration, workflow patterns)
- `consulting-transformation`: Tier 3 consulting covering Layer 3 (AI strategy, custom agent pipelines, integration architecture, embedded coaching)
- `thought-leadership`: Publishing the AppDevOps Manifesto to establish authority and generate inbound leads

### Modified Capabilities

- None (initial setup)
