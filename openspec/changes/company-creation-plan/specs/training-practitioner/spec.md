## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Tier 2 Practitioner Training Format
The training service SHALL provide a 3-day workshop format with a maximum of 6 participants per session.

#### Scenario: Three day workshop delivery
- **WHEN** the training is delivered
- **THEN** it SHALL consist of 24 hours of content (8 hours/day including breaks)
- **AND** no more than 6 participants SHALL attend

### Requirement: Tier 2 Layer 2 Focus
The training SHALL cover Layer 2 content: agent development, RAG systems, AI DevOps integration, and workflow patterns.

#### Scenario: Content covers agent development
- **WHEN** participants need to build AI agents
- **THEN** the training SHALL include agent architecture, tool use, and orchestration patterns
- **AND** SHALL cover multi-agent systems

#### Scenario: Content covers RAG systems
- **WHEN** participants need to integrate knowledge bases with LLMs
- **THEN** the training SHALL cover retrieval-augmented generation concepts, vector databases, and embedding strategies
- **AND** SHALL include hands-on RAG implementation exercises

#### Scenario: Content covers AI DevOps integration
- **WHEN** participants want to integrate AI into CI/CD pipelines
- **THEN** the training SHALL cover AI-enhanced testing, automated code review, and deployment strategies
- **AND** SHALL include monitoring and observability for AI systems

### Requirement: Tier 2 Pricing
The training SHALL be priced at $500 per person per day.

#### Scenario: Pricing calculation
- **WHEN** a company enrolls 4 participants for Tier 2
- **THEN** the total price SHALL be 4 × $500 × 3 days = $6,000
- **AND** payment terms SHALL be net 30

### Requirement: Tier 2 Materials
The training SHALL provide digital materials including slides, exercises, code examples, and reference architectures.

#### Scenario: Materials distribution
- **WHEN** the training begins
- **THEN** all participants SHALL receive digital access to slides
- **AND** code examples in a git repository
- **AND** reference architecture diagrams in PDF format
