## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Manifesto Publication Format
The thought leadership capability SHALL publish the AppDevOps Manifesto in both LinkedIn article format and PDF ebook format.

#### Scenario: LinkedIn article publication
- **WHEN** the manifesto is ready for publication
- **THEN** it SHALL be posted as a LinkedIn article with key excerpts
- **AND** SHALL include a call-to-action to download the full PDF

#### Scenario: PDF ebook creation
- **WHEN** the manifesto is published on LinkedIn
- **THEN** a professionally formatted PDF SHALL be created
- **AND** made available for download

### Requirement: Content Distribution
The thought leadership capability SHALL include regular LinkedIn posts (2-3x per week) on AppDevOps principles.

#### Scenario: Regular posting schedule
- **WHEN** the thought leadership campaign is active
- **THEN** content SHALL be posted at least 2 times per week
- **AND** SHALL include thought leadership, short posts, and engagement with AI discussions

### Requirement: Lead Capture
The thought leadership capability SHALL capture inbound leads from content consumption.

#### Scenario: Lead capture from PDF download
- **WHEN** a prospect downloads the PDF manifesto
- **THEN** their email address SHALL be captured
- **AND** they SHALL be added to a nurture sequence

#### Scenario: Lead capture from LinkedIn engagement
- **WHEN** a prospect messages via LinkedIn about the content
- **THEN** the interaction SHALL be logged for follow-up
- **AND** a discovery call SHALL be scheduled within 48 hours
