## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Tier 1 Foundation Training Format
The training service SHALL provide a 1-day workshop format with a maximum of 8 participants per session.

#### Scenario: Single day workshop delivery
- **WHEN** the training is delivered
- **THEN** it SHALL consist of 8 hours of content (including breaks)
- **AND** no more than 8 participants SHALL attend

### Requirement: Tier 1 Layer 1 Focus
The training SHALL cover Layer 1 content: AI tools basics, LLM fundamentals, prompt engineering, and AI-assisted coding introduction.

#### Scenario: Content covers AI tools overview
- **WHEN** participants ask about AI coding tools
- **THEN** the training SHALL include comparison of Copilot, Claude, and other relevant AI coding assistants
- **AND** SHALL explain when to use each tool

#### Scenario: Content covers LLM fundamentals
- **WHEN** participants need to understand how LLMs work
- **THEN** the training SHALL explain transformer architecture, tokens, and context windows
- **AND** SHALL cover limitations and best practices

#### Scenario: Content covers prompt engineering
- **WHEN** participants write prompts for AI systems
- **THEN** the training SHALL teach clear instruction writing, few-shot examples, and output formatting
- **AND** SHALL include hands-on exercises

### Requirement: Tier 1 Pricing
The training SHALL be priced at $400 per person per day.

#### Scenario: Pricing calculation
- **WHEN** a company enrolls 6 participants for Tier 1
- **THEN** the total price SHALL be 6 × $400 = $2,400
- **AND** payment terms SHALL be net 30

### Requirement: Tier 1 Materials
The training SHALL provide digital materials including slides, exercises, and reference guides.

#### Scenario: Materials distribution
- **WHEN** the training begins
- **THEN** all participants SHALL receive digital access to slides
- **AND** exercise files and reference guides in PDF format

### Requirement: Tier 1 Feedback Collection
The training SHALL collect participant feedback at the end of each session.

#### Scenario: Feedback collection
- **WHEN** the training concludes
- **THEN** participants SHALL complete a feedback form
- **AND** results SHALL be stored for continuous improvement
